Glossary


  1.  Accessibility Tools – Features such as text-to-speech, closed captioning, and visual aids that support diverse learners.
     
  2.  Gamification – The use of interactive lessons, quizzes, and rewards to enhance student engagement.
     
  3.  Multimodal Learning – An approach that integrates various forms of content delivery, including visual, auditory, and interactive methods.
     
  4.  Personalized Learning – Adaptive lesson plans that adjust based on a student's strengths and weaknesses.
     
  5.  Role-Based Access – A security feature that ensures users (students, teachers, parents) have appropriate permissions based on their role.
     
  6.  Secure Authentication – Measures to protect user privacy and maintain data security.
     
  7.  Virtual Tutoring – Online support resources designed to assist students outside of traditional classroom settings.
